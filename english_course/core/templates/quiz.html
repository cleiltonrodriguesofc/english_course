Class 3 Review Activity 📝

Test your knowledge on Pronouns, Verb To Be, and Wh- Questions!

Part 1: Pronoun Challenge

Select the correct pronoun for each sentence.

1. ______ am a student, but she is the teacher.
2. Do you like ______?
3. This is ______ house.
4. That car is not ______.
5. She talks to ______ every day.

Part 2: Sentence Transformation

Rewrite the sentences in Negative and Interrogative forms.

1. You are a student.
2. They are intelligent.

Part 3: Wh- Questions

Choose the correct word: Where, What, Who, How, When.

1. __________ are you from? (Answer: I am from Brazil)
2. __________ is your name? (Answer: My name is John)
3. __________ is that man? (Answer: That is the teacher)
