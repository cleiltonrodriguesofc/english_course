Adverbs of Frequency ⏱️

We use these words to say HOW OFTEN we do something.
They usually go BEFORE the action verb.
ALWAYS
(Sempre) - 100% of the time.
I always brush my teeth.
USUALLY
(Geralmente) - 80% of the time.
I usually do my homework in the afternoon.
SOMETIMES
(Às vezes) - 50% of the time.
I sometimes play soccer on Sundays.
NEVER
(Nunca) - 0% of the time.
I never drink coffee.

Putting It Together! 🧩

Let's combine Routine + Time + Frequency!
English Portuguese Listen
I always wake up at 7 AM. Eu sempre acordo às 7 da manhã.
I usually have lunch at noon. Eu geralmente almoço ao meio-dia.
I sometimes play games at night. Eu às vezes jogo na parte da noite.
I never go to bed late. Eu nunca vou dormir tarde.
