Important Verbs 🗣️

Use EAT for food, and DRINK for liquids. You can also use HAVE for both!
🍕
I eat pizza on weekends.
(Eu como pizza aos finais de semana.)
💧
She drinks water every day.
(Ela bebe água todo dia.)
🍳
I have breakfast at 8 AM.
(Eu tomo café da manhã às 8h.)

Ordering & Asking ❓

Question Answer Listen
What do you want to eat? I want a hamburger.
What do you want to drink? I want orange juice, please.
Can I have a coffee? Yes, here you go.
