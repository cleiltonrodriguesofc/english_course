Describing People 🗣️

👩
My mother is short. She has brown eyes.
(Minha mãe é baixa. Ela tem olhos castanhos.)
🧔
My uncle is tall. He has short hair.
(Meu tio é alto. Ele tem cabelo curto.)
👦
I am young. I have curly hair.
(Eu sou jovem. Eu tenho cabelo cacheado.)

Questions & Answers ❓

Use "Who is he/she?" to ask about family, and "What does he/she look like?" for appearance!
Question Answer Listen
Who is he? He is my brother.
What does she look like? She is tall and thin.
Does he have blue eyes? No, he has brown eyes.
