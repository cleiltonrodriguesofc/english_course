Expressing Likes ❤️

When you like something, you can say: I like, I love, or I enjoy.
👍
I like soccer.
(Eu gosto de futebol.)
❤️
I love pizza!
(Eu amo pizza!)
😎
I enjoy reading books.
(Eu aprecio/curto ler livros.)

Expressing Dislikes 💔

When you don't like something, say: I don't like or I hate.
👎
I don't like salad.
(Eu não gosto de salada.)
👿
I hate tennis.
(Eu odeio tênis.)

Questions & Answers ❓

Use "Do you like...?" to ask questions!
Question Answer Listen
Do you like ice cream? Yes, I do!
Do you like washing the dishes? No, I don't.
What is your favorite sport? My favorite sport is soccer.
