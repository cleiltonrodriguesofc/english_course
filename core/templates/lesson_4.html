Expressing Likes ❤️

When we talk about hobbies, we use -ING after LIKE.

I like reading.

She loves dancing.

They enjoy swimming.

Note: We use the gerund (-ing) because the verb acts like a noun!

Love and Hate ❤️ 😡

Love (++++):
"I love playing video games."

Don't mind (+/-):
"I don't mind cooking." (É ok)

Hate (----):
"I hate running in the rain."

Try to say your own preference out loud!
