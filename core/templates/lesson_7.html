Talking About Your Week 🗣️

💡 Tip: Use ON + day of the week: on Monday, on weekends.
English Portuguese Listen
I go to school on Monday. Eu vou para a escola na segunda.
I play video games on Saturday. Eu jogo video game no sábado.
I have English class on Tuesday. Eu tenho aula de inglês na terça.
I play soccer on weekends. Eu jogo futebol nos finais de semana.

Events in the Year 🎉

English Portuguese Listen
My birthday is in July. Meu aniversário é em julho.
My summer vacation is in January. Minhas férias de verão são em janeiro.
Children's Day is in October. O Dia das Crianças é em outubro.
Christmas is in December. O Natal é em dezembro.

Telling the Time ⏰

💡 Tip: Use AT for time! AM (before 12:00) and PM (after 12:00).
07:00
Seven o'clock
"I wake up at 7 o'clock."
08:15
Eight fifteen
"I go to school at 8:15."
03:30
Three thirty (PM)
"I do my homework at 3:30 pm."
10:00
Ten o'clock (PM)
"I go to sleep at 10 pm."

Seasons of the Year 🍂

⚠️ Brazil's seasons are OPPOSITE tracking from Europe/USA! While it's Summer here (December), it's Winter in the USA!
