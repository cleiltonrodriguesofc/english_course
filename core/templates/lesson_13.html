Describing Clothes 🗣️

Use "I am wearing..." (Eu estou vestindo/usando...)
👕
I am wearing a blue T-shirt.
(Eu estou vestindo uma camiseta azul.)
👗
She is wearing a red dress.
(Ela está vestindo um vestido vermelho.)
👖
He is wearing black pants.
(Ele está vestindo calça preta.)
*Pants is plural, so we don't use 'a'.

Questions & Answers ❓

Question Answer Listen
What are you wearing? I am wearing a blue jacket.
What is he wearing? He is wearing white sneakers.
Are you wearing glasses? Yes, I am.
