Going Places 🗺️

English Portuguese Listen
Where are you going? Aonde você está indo?
I am going to the park. Eu estou indo para o parque.
How do you go to school? Como você vai para a escola?
I go to school by bus. Eu vou para a escola de ônibus.

Prepositions of Place 📍

These words tell us WHERE something is located!
IN
(Dentro) The keys are in the car.
ON
(Em cima de) The book is on the table.
NEXT TO
(Ao lado de) The school is next to the park.
BEHIND
(Atrás de) The cat is behind the tree.
UNDER
(Embaixo de) The dog is under the bed.
